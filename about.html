The Scheme

PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana

Launched in February 2024, this flagship scheme aims to provide free solar power to 10 million households across India.

Feb 2024 — Launch

Prime Minister launches PM Surya Ghar with a ₹75,021 crore outlay targeting 1 crore households by March 2027.

Portal Opens — pmsuryagarh.gov.in

National online portal launched for consumer registration, vendor selection, and subsidy disbursement tracking.

1.3M+ Applicants Enrolled

Within months, over 1.3 million households register, exceeding initial monthly targets.

Solar Yojna AI Launched

We built this AI assistant to help the remaining 8.7 million eligible households understand and navigate the scheme effortlessly.
